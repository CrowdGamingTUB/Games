About [back]

Tap the black tiles is a clone of Piano tiles : don't tap the white tiles originally designed for Firefox OS, but available on any device that can run a web browser.

It is developed by Nicolas Hurtubise (aka me) during his free time. The original game is not free (as in freedom) and comes with advertisement (which I find annoying).

If you have ideas for new modes or for anything that could improve the game, let me know !

This game is distributed under the MIT license, and is therefore free and open source software. You can contribute to it directly on Github.
